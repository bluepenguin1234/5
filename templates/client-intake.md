# Client Intake — Frontpage Studio

Everything we need from a new client before the build starts. This document is the master list of questions. The recommended way to collect it from clients is below.

---

## How to use this document

### Recommended: Turn it into a Google Form (10 min, then reuse forever)

1. Go to **forms.google.com** → blank form → name it "Frontpage Studio New Client Intake"
2. Copy each question below into the form, picking the matching question type (short answer / paragraph / multiple choice / file upload)
3. Mark file uploads (logo, photos) as **File upload** question type. Tick "Allow only specific file types" → image (and PDF for logo). Cap file size at 10MB. Allow multiple files where indicated.
4. In Settings → Responses → toggle on **Collect email addresses** and **Link to a Google Sheet** so every submission lands in one spreadsheet automatically
5. Click **Send** → copy the form link. That link is what you paste into the post-deposit email.

**Note for clients:** Google Forms requires the client to be signed into a Google account to upload files. Most small business owners already have a Gmail. If a client doesn't, fall back to the email option below.

### Alternative: Send this as an email

Copy the intake questions (Sections 1 to 12 below) into an email. Ask the client to reply with their answers and attach the logo plus photos as attachments. Less tidy but works for any client. Use this if Google Form upload is a problem.

### Alternative: Send this document as a Google Doc

Paste the intake content into a new Google Doc → click Share → "Anyone with the link can edit" → send link to client. They fill in answers inline and attach photos through email.

## Section 6 — Logo and Photos (file uploads)

### Logo
- Upload your logo (PNG with transparent background preferred, or PDF, or high-res JPG)
- If you don't have one: check "No logo yet" — we'll use a clean text-based logo at no charge, or design a simple icon logo for an additional $149

### Photos
Upload 10 to 20 photos. Phone photos are fine. We resize and optimize them.

What we look for:
- **Exterior** (storefront, sign, building)
- **Interior** (the space where you work or serve)
- **Products or services in action** (food on plates, hair being cut, truck on a job)
- **Owner / team** (faces build trust)
- **Anything else** that says "this is what we do"

If you don't have enough photos: check "Use stock for [category]" and we pull from free Unsplash photography to fill the gaps.
