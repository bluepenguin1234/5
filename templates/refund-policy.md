# Refund and Cancellation Policy

Frontpage Studio
Effective: 2026

This policy explains what happens to your money at each stage of a Frontpage Studio engagement. It applies to every client and is incorporated by reference into the Master Service Agreement.

---

## Build fee refunds

The Business Presence Package build fee is $599, split into a $299.50 deposit and a $299.50 launch balance.

| Stage | Refund |
|---|---|
| You paid the deposit but have not yet submitted your intake form | **Full deposit refund** ($299.50). No work has begun. |
| You submitted the intake form but the staging preview has not yet been delivered | **50% deposit refund** ($149.75). We have started but not delivered. |
| The staging preview has been delivered | **Deposit is non-refundable.** Work has been delivered for your review. |
| You paid the launch balance and the site is live | **Build fee is non-refundable.** The work has been completed and delivered. |

---

## Ongoing Care Plan refunds

The Ongoing Care Plan is $79/month with a mandatory 12-month minimum term beginning at launch.

### During months 1 to 12 (the minimum term)

- **Care Plan months already paid are non-refundable.** They cover services already rendered (hosting, security monitoring, edits performed, support availability).
- **If you cancel during this period,** the remaining months through month 12 become immediately due and payable.

### After month 12

- You can cancel any time with 30 days' written notice to brian@frontpagestudio.com.
- Months paid after cancellation notice but before the 30-day notice period ends are non-refundable.

### Service failure exception

If Frontpage Studio fails to keep your site operating for **five or more consecutive business days** during the Care Plan term, you may cancel without penalty and without payment of remaining minimum-term months. This excludes outages caused by:

- Third-party providers we do not control (Vercel, Namecheap, Formspree, Google)
- Force-majeure events (natural disasters, government actions, internet-wide failures)
- Anything caused by your own actions (cancelling your domain, changing DNS without telling us)

---

## Domain pass-through

The domain registration fee is a direct pass-through to the registrar (Namecheap). It is non-refundable once the domain has been registered, because we have already paid the registrar on your behalf and they do not refund.

If you change your mind about the domain name within 24 hours of telling us, we may be able to switch it for you depending on the registrar's grace period. After 24 hours, the original domain stays registered to you and any new domain is billed separately.

---

## Add-ons

Add-ons (extra pages, FAQ section, custom logo, e-commerce, rush delivery, extra revisions) follow the same refund rules as the build fee. If the add-on work has not yet started, you get a full refund. If it has been delivered, no refund.

---

## How to request a refund

Email brian@frontpagestudio.com with:

1. Your business name and reference number
2. The reason you are requesting a refund
3. The payment(s) you want refunded

We respond within one business day. Approved refunds are processed via the same payment method you used, typically within 5 to 10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

---

## Disputes

If we cannot agree on a refund, the dispute-resolution process in Section 13 of the Master Service Agreement applies (good-faith negotiation, then binding arbitration in [COUNTY], [STATE]).

We have never had to invoke that process. We would rather find a fair outcome by talking it through.
