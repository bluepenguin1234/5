This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Frontpage Studio ("Provider") and the Client identified above ("Client"). The accompanying proposal dated [DATE] ("Proposal"), attached and incorporated by reference, defines the specific scope, deliverables, and pricing for the work to be performed.

1.Scope of Work

Provider will deliver the Business Presence Package described in the Proposal: a three-page website (Home, Services, Contact), mobile-friendly design, contact form with click-to-call, reviews section, photo gallery, business hours and service area display, Google Maps embed, email signup form, Google Business Profile setup and optimization, search-ready setup (page titles, meta descriptions, consistent NAP, LocalBusiness schema markup), and one round of revisions. Any work beyond this scope is governed by Section 4 below.

2.Fees and Payment

Business Presence Package: $599 one-time build fee. Payable as $299.50 at signing (deposit) and $299.50 at launch (balance).

Ongoing Care Plan: $79 per month, beginning at launch. The first month is collected with the balance payment at launch. The Care Plan is required with this build for a minimum of twelve (12) consecutive months. After the initial twelve-month term, the Care Plan continues month-to-month and may be cancelled by Client with thirty (30) days' written notice. Care Plan covers: hosting, security updates, website edits, backup and maintenance, and email support.

Domain: Provider will register a domain on Client's behalf through Namecheap if Client does not already own one. The registrar's actual annual cost (approximately $12 to $15 for `.com`) will be passed through to Client at cost as a separate line item on the launch invoice and re-billed annually at cost. There is no markup and no setup fee. Client is the registered owner of the domain.

Add-ons: Optional add-ons listed in the Proposal (extra pages at $75 each, FAQ section at $75, custom logo at $149, e-commerce at $399, rush delivery at +$200, additional revision rounds at $75 each) are billed in addition to the package fee.

First-year total: $1,547 ($599 build + $948 Care Plan), excluding domain pass-through and optional add-ons.

All payments are processed via Stripe. Late Care Plan payments are subject to a $15 late fee after seven (7) days and Provider may suspend service after thirty (30) days of non-payment.

3.Timeline

Provider will deliver the completed site for Client review within seven (7) business days of receiving both: (a) the executed deposit payment, and (b) Client's completed intake form including all required content (business information, photos, logo, reviews). The seven-day clock starts at the later of these two events. Delays in providing content extend the timeline by an equivalent number of days.

4.Revisions and Out-of-Scope Work

The Business Presence Package includes one (1) round of revisions before launch. A "round" consists of one consolidated list of changes submitted via email after Client has reviewed the staging preview. Provider will implement all revisions within forty-eight (48) hours of receipt.

Additional revision rounds are billed at $75 each. Out-of-scope work (new pages, new features, redesign after approval, content writing beyond what Client provides) is billed at $75 per hour or by flat-fee agreement reached in writing before the work begins.

5.Care Plan: Cancellation and Termination

During the initial twelve-month term: The Care Plan is non-cancellable. If Client terminates the Care Plan before twelve months have elapsed, all remaining monthly fees through the end of the initial term become immediately due and payable.

After the initial twelve-month term: Client may cancel with thirty (30) days' written notice to brian@frontpagestudio.com. No early-termination fee applies.

Provider termination for cause: Provider may terminate the Care Plan with thirty (30) days' notice if Client (a) fails to pay for thirty (30) days or more after invoice, (b) materially breaches this Agreement, or (c) engages in abusive conduct toward Provider. Remaining minimum-term fees remain due in either case.

Service failure clause: If Provider fails to maintain the site in working order for five (5) or more consecutive business days during the Care Plan term (excluding force-majeure events covered in Section 11), Client may terminate the Care Plan without payment of remaining months.

6.Refunds

Deposit refund schedule:

  • Before Client submits the intake form: full deposit refund (no work performed).
  • After intake submitted but before staging preview delivered: 50% deposit refund ($149.75).
  • After staging preview delivered: deposit is non-refundable (work delivered).
  • After launch: build fee is non-refundable.

Care Plan refunds: Care Plan months already paid are non-refundable. The "service failure clause" in Section 5 is the only exception.

7.Intellectual Property

Final deliverables: Upon final payment, Client owns the completed website code, content, and the registered domain outright. Client may host the site elsewhere at any time after the initial twelve-month Care Plan term concludes; Provider will hand over all files within twenty-four (24) hours of written request.

Domain ownership: Client is always the registered owner of the domain, regardless of which party paid the registration fee. Provider may hold administrative control of the registrar account during the build and Care Plan term for technical management; full account credentials will be transferred to Client on request at any time.

Pre-existing materials: Provider retains ownership of underlying frameworks, design systems, and code libraries used to build the site. Client receives a perpetual license to use these as embedded in the delivered site.

Portfolio rights: Provider may display the completed site in its portfolio, case studies, and marketing materials, identifying Client by name and including screenshots, unless Client specifically opts out in writing.

8.Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to: (a) provide accurate and complete business information through the intake form; (b) respond to Provider's review requests within forty-eight (48) hours during the build period; (c) submit revision requests as a single consolidated email within the seventy-two (72) hour review window after staging preview; (d) pay Care Plan invoices on time; (e) not request work that is illegal, defamatory, discriminatory, or that infringes third-party intellectual property.

9.Confidentiality

Each party will hold the other party's confidential business information in confidence and use it only for purposes of performing this Agreement. This obligation does not apply to information that is publicly available, independently developed, or required to be disclosed by law.
