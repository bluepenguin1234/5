Our Story

A table for every occasion.

Maplewood Kitchen was born from a simple belief: that the best meals happen when honest ingredients are treated with quiet skill. Chef Maria Harmon built this kitchen around local farms, family recipes, and a relentless commitment to flavor over fanfare.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone or just craving a glass of something good on a Tuesday, we keep a seat ready for you.
