About Tucker Plumbing
FAMILY-OWNED.
LOCALLY TRUSTED.

Tucker Plumbing was founded in 2008 by Dave Tucker, a second-generation plumber who grew up learning the trade from his father. What started as a one-truck operation has grown into a team of eight licensed plumbers — but we've never lost the personal touch that makes our customers keep coming back.

We serve Columbus and all surrounding communities including Dublin, Westerville, Grove City, Hilliard, Powell, and New Albany.
