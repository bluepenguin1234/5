About Sharp & Co.
YOUR BARBER
FOR LIFE.

We opened Sharp & Co. in 2012 because we believed Columbus deserved a barbershop that took the craft seriously. Not a quick-cut chain — a real shop where your barber remembers your name, your style, and how you take your coffee.

Every barber at Sharp & Co. is licensed, trained in modern and classic techniques, and obsessed with the details. We use straight razors the right way, source our products from small-batch makers, and keep the shop clean enough to eat off the floor.
