Questions
The things people ask before saying yes.
Do I own the website?
+
Yes — 100%. Once you pay in full, the website is yours. The code, the content, the domain. We don't hold anything hostage, ever. After the 12-month care plan ends, if you decide to host elsewhere we hand over all your files within 24 hours.
What does the $79/month cover?
+
It covers hosting, security updates, website edits, backup and maintenance, and support. The care plan is required with every build for a minimum of 12 months — that's how we can offer a low one-time build fee while still keeping your site running smoothly. After 12 months it's month-to-month, cancel anytime.
Do I need to know anything about tech?
+
Not a thing. We handle design, development, domain setup, hosting, and launch. All you do is fill out a 10-minute form and review a preview link. That's it.
How will people find me on Google?
+
Every site includes search-ready setup — page titles, meta descriptions, consistent name/address/phone, local schema markup, and Google Business Profile setup. Results typically start appearing within 2–4 weeks of launch.
What if I already have a logo?
+
Great — send it over and we'll use it. If you don't have one, we'll use a clean text-based logo at no extra charge, or design a simple icon logo for an additional $149.
What does "1 revision round" mean?
+
After we send you the preview, you send us one consolidated list of changes. We make them all at once. The package includes one revision round. Additional rounds after that are $75 each.
