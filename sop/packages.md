**Required with every build. Not optional. 12-month minimum term.**

After 12 months the plan converts to month-to-month and the client can cancel with 30 days' written notice.

**What's included:**

| Deliverable | Details |
|---|---|
| Hosting | Site hosted on Vercel — fast, global CDN, free SSL |
| Security updates | SSL renewal, dependency patches, monitoring |
| Website edits | Text changes, new photos, updated hours, seasonal promos — budget ~1 hr/month |
| Backup / maintenance | Site files versioned in GitHub; restorable any time |
| Support | Same-day weekday response by email |

**Term & billing:**
- **First 12 months:** Mandatory. Cannot be cancelled without paying the remaining balance.
- **After 12 months:** Month-to-month. Cancel with 30 days' notice.
- **First month** collected at launch alongside the build balance ($79).
- **Months 2–12** billed automatically each month on the launch-date anniversary.
- **First-year client commitment:** $948.

**Cost-to-deliver notes:**
- Hosting alone costs you $0 on Vercel's free tier — the $79 is nearly pure profit
- Budget ~1 hr/month per client for edits; if a client routinely exceeds this, offer the 2-hr upgrade at $129/month or charge overage at $75/hr
- If a client tries to cancel during the 12 months: politely remind them of the agreement; remaining months are due in full
- If they cancel after month 12: hand over files cleanly within 24 hours — no hard feelings, no domain hostage

## WHAT TO COLLECT BEFORE YOU BUILD

Get these from the client before starting:

- [ ] Business name, tagline (if any)
- [ ] Phone number, email, address
- [ ] Hours of operation + service area (cities/zip codes you serve)
- [ ] Services list with descriptions
- [ ] 10–20 photos (or permission to pull from Google Maps / use stock)
- [ ] 3–6 customer reviews/testimonials (text + first name)
- [ ] Social media handles
- [ ] Any existing logo files
- [ ] Domain login (if they already own one) or they buy one with your guidance
- [ ] Color/style preferences (or send them 3 examples and ask them to pick)
- [ ] Google account info for GBP setup (or set up a new one with them)

Tell clients: **"The faster you get me the content, the faster your site launches."**
