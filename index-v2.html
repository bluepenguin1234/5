The questions
that come up before yes.

Six honest answers. If something is missing, ask on the call.
Do I own the website? +
Yes, 100%. Once you pay in full, the website is yours. The code, the content, the domain. We do not hold anything hostage. After the 12 month care plan ends, if you choose to host elsewhere we hand over all your files within 24 hours.
What does the $79 a month cover? +
Hosting, security updates, website edits, backup and maintenance, and support. The care plan is required for the first 12 months. That is how we offer the low upfront build fee while keeping your site live and current. After 12 months it is month to month, cancel anytime.
Do I need to know anything about tech? +
Not a thing. We handle design, development, domain setup, hosting, and launch. All you do is fill out a ten minute form and review a preview link.
How will people find me on Google? +
Every build includes the search ready setup: page titles, meta descriptions, consistent name and address, local schema markup, and Google Business Profile setup. Results typically start appearing within 2 to 4 weeks of launch.
What if I already have a logo? +
Great. Send it over and we use it. If you do not have one, we use a clean text based logo at no extra charge, or design a simple icon logo for an additional $149.
What does “1 revision round” mean? +
After we send you the preview, you send us one consolidated list of changes. We make them all at once. The package includes one round. Additional rounds are $75 each.
